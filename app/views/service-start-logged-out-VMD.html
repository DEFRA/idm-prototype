Assurance for a limited company

To enrol your organisation with the VMD service you need to:

  • agree to VMD policies
  • tell us your market authority holder number (if known)
  • nominate another person in your organisation who we can contact to authorise the application

You need to use a Government Gateway account to register for this service

We ask you to use or create a Government Gateway account to register with this service, as well as other services provided by Defra and related agencies and public bodies.

Having one set of sign in details (a Government Gateway user ID and password) will save you time. We will store basic information about you (and your business, if relevant) so that you do not have to re-enter the information each time you want to use a new online service.

To register for our online services you will be guided to:

  1. Enter your Government Gateway user ID and password if you already have an account. If not, you will be guided through the process of creating one.
  2. Tell us some information about you and how you want to use our online services.
  3. Visit the [service name] service to [service-specific content, if relevant, then] complete your registration.

Before you start

You will need:

  • your email address, phone number and postal address
  • your Government Gateway user ID and password (if you already have an account, if not you will be guided to create one)
  • the main contact details for your organisation (for example, head office contact details)
  • the Companies House number for the limited company you want to enrol
