Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website. We use 2 types of cookie on this site:

  • Cookies that are strictly necessary
  • Cookies that measure service usage

Introductory cookie message

You may see a pop-up welcome message when you first visit. A cookie is stored so that your computer knows you've seen it and knows not to show it again

Cookies that are strictly necessary

These cookies are essential to make this service run effectively and to help us comply with data protection laws. You cannot turn these cookies off.

Google reCAPTCHA cookies

We use Google reCAPTCHA on this site to make sure that information entered (for example, in forms) has been entered by a human and not by automated software. Some of the data collected by reCAPTCHA cookies is forwarded to Google. For more information, refer to the Google Privacy Policy and the Google Terms of Service.

Cookies that measure service usage

These cookies track the pages you visit here, how long you spend on each one and what you select. You can turn these cookies on or off.

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Application Insights to collect information about how you use the service. We do this to make sure that the service is meeting your needs and to help us make improvements.

We do not collect or store your personal information (for example, your name or address) and we do not allow Google or Microsoft to use our data or share it with anyone else.
