Before you start

The registration process consists of several steps. You can return to the application at anytime should you need to.

1. Login with or create a Government Gateway Account

You need a Government Gateway account to use this service. This also allows you to access other Gov.uk and Defra services.

By registering for this service you will become the administrator which will allow you to manage other users.

You can login with an existing Government Gateway account if it was created for the company or organisation you are representing.

2. Capture your details

You need to provide details about yourself as you will be the administrator for the service.

If you are representing a company or organsiation you will need to provide information such as your Company House registered business details.

3. Service specific information

The following information is also required.

  • Some info like billing address
  • Some info like the DUNS number

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
